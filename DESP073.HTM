From: ALPHA::COOLIDGE

        The plot seems to be thickening. If you didn't hear, Dave
Rodgers, the Distributed Systems Hardware Engineering group manager
submitted his resignation around July 5th or 6th (announced on the
6th, I think) to take the position of vice-president of Engineering
for Sequel Computer Systems of Portland, Oregon. Sequel is a start-up
comprised of 11 former Intel employees. Its marketing veep is Larry
Wade, former VAX OEM marketing manager.

        Two days after Dave Rodgers announced his resignation, Dave's
boss, Bernie LaCroute, Distributed Systems manager, also announced
that he was leavingfor Sun Microsystems. I think that Sun is Sunoco' s
answer to Exxon Office Systems, but I'm not sure - haven't seen any
ads yet.

        Dave was essentially in charge of all of the Ethernet and
traditional comm gear development programs. Bernie owned all of that
plus DECnet software.

        Sounds like BJ is going to be busy...
