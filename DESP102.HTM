Reuters WOENSDRECHT, Netherlands -- Dutch antinuclear protestors yesterday
sowed marijuana seeds around the 11-mile perimeter fence of an airbase here,

missiles.

Police said two dozen members of a group, calling itself Dutch Cannabis

could not reach.

action against seven US Air Force servicemen in Britain for allegedly
smoking marijuana.

later be unable to resist the temptation and get sent home," the spokesman
said.

        Recently, I'm told, IBM found that their manufacturing
capacity was not going to be able to produce 100,000 transistors that
was needed for a new personal computer.  They would have to turn to an
outside source for this supply.  They selected a highly reputable,
quality conscious Japanese company.

        During their negotiations, IBM told this Japanese company that
their AQL (Acceptable Quality Level) was 95% -- they would accept 5
parts per hundred as defective.  The Japanese couldn't quite
comprehend this, but as dedicated vendors, they attempted to comply.

        IBM received their shipment in two packages, a large and a
small.  An accompanying invoice explained that, to make it easier for
them, the large package contains the 95,000 good transistors and the
small package contains, you guessed it, the 5000 defective
transistors.

Let me be the first of many who respond that DEC's "official corporate mail
system" does indeed support such things as store and forward, routing, priority
mail, etc.  However, VMS added their hack mail to their kit and only years
later will it be possible to send mail from VMS mail to the corporate mail
system.  The reverse has been possible for years.  Note that the corporate
mail system has several user interfaces, (the VMS mail system has only
one and allows only one).  The transport of messages is handled by the Message
Router which has a well defined interface.  The user interfaces include DMAIL,
and several from the various "office" products including ALL-IN-1.  EMS is
being replaced by MTS which uses the MR and ALL-IN-1.
