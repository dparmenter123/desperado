will check out the virtual masthead, you will see John Covert's name up

BOOKIE::NEILSEN. Send mailing list requests to CASTOR::DESPERADO_REQUEST

against hyphens.

only thing place you can put your contribution that we won't find it
is into the trunk of a hollow tree, and I have a few squirrels on the
payroll that are working on that.

So hang by your thumbs, write if you get work, and we'll see you at Tom's
going-away party!
