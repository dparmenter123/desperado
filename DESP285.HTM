 The decidedly low-rent Ginsana ginseng capsules has an ad asking
 folks what they would do if they had more energy (presumably from
 popping their pills.  All sorts of mundanity results -- spend more
 time with the kids, play basketball, get another job -- then, right
 at the end, a voice pops up stating that he would play Cajun Bach.  A
 cello cranks up a little Bach in the background that quickly morphs
 into two or three bars of Cajun sounds.
