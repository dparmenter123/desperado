From: MILVAX::DAHLBERG
To:   ALIEN::PARMENTER
Subject: texts

Here's one minor comment on the Japanese and their new textbooks.

Petitions were circulated thru DEC by our Oriental employees (mostly
Chinese) and were then sent to the Japanese Consulate in Boston.
The petitions specifically objected to the pro-Japan slant of these
textbooks, the suddenly elimination of blame from the Japanese govern-
ment of Japan's part as an aggressor in WW II, and the notion that
any action against the rioting Chinese public (as in Nanking) was
strictly a police action.  As far as I know, the organization has
received no offical acknowledgement from the Japanese government.
Similar petitions have been instigated in other countries and now
pressure has been brought to bear on these governments to make an
official statement to Japan, calling for the truth to be written down
for the Japanese children to learn from.

As my friend told me, when she came to me with the petition, she felt
it wasn't a case of trying to "blame Japan for the atrocities they
committed" as much as it was to educate their children to make
sure that it is understood that this was a genocide of the Chinese
people.  She wanted to make sure that the children did not grow up
thinking that what Japan did to the Chinese was an acceptable way
of dealing with a culture and a people.

Er..........   I am now getting off the soapbox......................
