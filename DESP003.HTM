I received my benefits packet today in the mail.  In light of the recent
announcement regarding salaries and freezes, it certainly feels like having
someone rub salt in the wound when I look at the package outlining my
benefits.  I heard an estimate that it cost in the area of $100 to produce
each of these elegant (and useless) packages.

I would be quite satisfied to have my benefits copied on newsprint and see
a few extra dollars in my check.  If money is so tight, why throw it away
on trivialities?  I understand that times are tough economically, but I'd
rather not be insulted by things like this while I "bite the bullet."

Maybe we should be biting the benefits package.  Something's rotten in
Denmark.
